About Us

Where Nature Meets Comfort

Nestled in the serene surroundings of the world-renowned Sinharaja Rainforest, Sinharaja Forest Edge offers a peaceful escape in the charming village of Deniyaya, right by the Mederipitiya entrance

Our resort blends comfort with authenticity — providing essential modern amenities while preserving the simplicity of village life by the forest. With warm Sri Lankan hospitality, we welcome all travelers, whether you are a tourist, backpacker, or on a short or long stay, to experience a unique harmony of nature and relaxation.
